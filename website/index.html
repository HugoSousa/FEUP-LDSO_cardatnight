About

Order from your phone, avoid waiting lines.

So, you're dancing with your friends, but you need a drink. But you hate that moment, all that confusion when you have to wait around the bar and shout out loud what you want?

Don't worry!


With card@night, ordering a drink becomes much more confortable. With a few clicks, you can order what you want and you'll be notified when it's ready.

Your order will have a password associated and you just have to tell it when you go get your drink at the bar. Or tell your password to a friend and let him do it for you.

When the night is over, you pay at the exit, just like a regular consumption card.

Advantages for all


But hey! There's advantages for all, not just the consumers.


If you're the owner of an establishment, providing this service will be an attractive factor to your customers. Plus you get relevant information and statistics from the usage of the application.

Our platform is composed by 3 different applications.

Mobile - Client

If you're going to enter an adherent establishment, just download the application below. Download available for Android 2.3+, so far.

Mobile - Doorman

The accounts/application for doorman are provided by card@night after you get in touch with us. This application simply allows the doorman to read the entrance and exit codes, assuring the security of access control.

Website - Employee / Manager

The accounts for employee/manager are provided by card@night after you get in touch with us. Here you can manage the payments, incoming orders and products. The manager has access to statistics about the establishment.
